The Best Movies According to Mishti

My top 3 movies of all-time

Final Destination

Final Destination is a 2000 American supernatural horror film directed by James Wong and written by Wong and Glen Morgan. It is the first installment in the Final Destination film series. The film stars Devon Sawa, Ali Larter, Kerr Smith, and Kristen Cloke. The plot follows a group of people who cheat death after one of them has a premonition of a plane explosion.

Raid 2

Raid 2 is a really good action movie that I watched recently. It is a sequel to the original Raid movie and continues the story of the main character.

Jigra

Jigra is a movie based on the relationship of a brother and sister, where brother gets caught because of his cousin's mistake and the sister tries to save him. The movie is really good and I would recommend it to everyone.
